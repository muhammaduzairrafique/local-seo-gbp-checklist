# Local SEO & Google Business Profile Checklist

A practical checklist for improving local visibility across Google Search, Google Maps, and the Local Pack.

## What This Checklist Covers

- Google Business Profile categories
- Business description
- Services and service areas
- NAP consistency
- Reviews and review responses
- Photos and profile completeness
- Local citations
- Local landing pages
- Internal linking
- Local keyword targeting
- Competitor gap review

## Purpose

This checklist helps local businesses identify what may be blocking better visibility, calls, direction requests, bookings, and local leads.
